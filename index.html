      New Home Purchase Loan

      This loan is for individuals looking to buy a new flat from a builder or a ready-built house (resale property). We connect you with the best banks for a quick and easy loan process.

      Key Loan Information

      Interest Rate (ROI)

      8.50% p.a. onwards*

      Processing Fee (PF)

      0.5% onwards*

      Loan Tenure

      Up to 30 Years

      Loan Amount

      Up to 90% of Property Value

      *T&C Apply. Rates depend on the applicant's profile.

      Required Documents

      For Salaried: ID Proof (PAN, Aadhaar), last 3 months' salary slips, 6 months' bank statement, Form 16.

      Family Floater Health Plan

      Secure your entire family's health under a single, affordable premium. The sum insured floats among all covered members, ensuring comprehensive protection for everyone.

      Key Features & Benefits

      • Single Premium: One premium for the entire family, making it more affordable than individual plans.
      • Shared Sum Insured: Any member can utilize the full sum insured during a medical emergency.
      • Cashless Hospitalization: Access to a wide network of hospitals for cashless treatment.
      • Tax Benefits: Save tax under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act.
